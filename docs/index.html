What Paglu Does :)

One extension. Three superpowers.

Scan Any Webpage

Visit your LinkedIn, portfolio, or any page with your data. AI extracts 50+ facts — name, skills, work history, projects, certificates, everything.

Upload Resume / PDF

Upload your resume or documents. Gemini's multimodal vision reads PDFs and images directly — no OCR needed.

Auto-Fill Any Form

Open a job application, click Fill. AI maps your memory to fields, clicks "Add Experience" buttons, writes personalized answers, and verifies everything.

Dynamic Memory

Facts stored locally in IndexedDB. Search, edit, or delete any memory. Categories: work, education, projects, skills, achievements, volunteering, and more.

Framework-Aware

Works with React, Vue, Angular, Svelte, MUI, Ant Design, and custom ARIA components. Uses native prototype setters to bypass controlled inputs.

Bring Your Own Key

No server, no subscription. Use Google AI Studio (free), NVIDIA, OpenRouter, or any OpenAI-compatible API. Data stays on your machine.

How It Works

Three steps to never manually fill a form again.

1

Build Memory

Scan your LinkedIn, upload your resume, or visit any page with your info. AI extracts every detail into structured memory.

2

Open a Form

Navigate to any job application, registration form, or profile page you need to fill out.

3

Click Fill

The AI reads the form, maps your data to fields, clicks buttons to add sections, fills everything, then verifies.
